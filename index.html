Project Overview

Key Features

  • Smart requests: natural‑language requests (e.g., “I need 3 days off next week”), with policy‑aware suggestions and remaining balance checks.
  • Conflict detection: warns about overlapping absences, critical deadlines, or WIP limits on your team.
  • Multi‑policy engine: supports annual leave, sick leave, parental leave, compensatory time, and custom accrual rules.
  • Approvals & workflows: single or multi‑step approval rules, delegation, and escalation paths.
  • Calendar integration: two‑way sync with Google Calendar / Outlook; imports country public holidays automatically.
  • Team capacity views: heatmaps and weekly availability to help managers plan coverage.
  • Analytics: usage trends, burnout risk indicators, fairness metrics, and forecasted capacity gaps.
  • Notifications: email/Slack/Teams alerts for requests, approvals, changes, and upcoming returns.
  • Security & compliance: SSO/OAuth2, RBAC, audit logs, data retention controls.

User Flows

Employee

  1. Open assistant and type “Need 2 days off next month.”
  2. AI suggests optimal dates and shows balance & policy warnings.
  3. Submit request; calendar preview appears; notifications sent.

Manager

  1. Review requests with capacity/conflict indicators.
  2. Approve, request changes, or delegate.
  3. Team calendar and coverage heatmap update in real‑time.

HR

  1. Define policies, accrual rates, carry‑over, and blackout periods.
  2. Run monthly accruals and exports for payroll.
  3. Audit logs & analytics dashboards for compliance.
